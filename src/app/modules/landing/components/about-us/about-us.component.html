About Us
PT. Sumber Rezeki Areca, a subsidiary of PT Bintang Selamanya, is a leading agricultural company based in Jambi, Indonesia. Established in 2015, our company has grown to become the biggest producer of betel nut in the region and has expanded its reach to export the product to Pakistan, India, and Bangladesh.
In addition to being a leading exporter of betel nuts, we are also committed to preserving the environment through sustainable farming practices. We continuously strive towards minimizing our carbon footprint by optimizing resource utilization, reducing waste generation, and adopting eco-friendly techniques.
As a company, we understand the vital role that agriculture plays in the global economy and are committed to making a positive impact. We are proud to be a part of Indonesia's growing agricultural sector and will continue to work towards expanding our presence and reach in the global market.
In conclusion, PT. Sumber Rezeki Areca stands as a symbol of excellence, integrity, and sustainability in the betel nut industry. With our strong foundation, experienced team, and unwavering commitment to quality, we are confident in our ability to continue setting new standards for agricultural practices and exports.
